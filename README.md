# Stakeholder Mapping – 360-Degree Digitalization of Bank Services

## Course
Critical Thinking, Design Thinking, Leadership and Teamwork

## Task
Task 09 – Stakeholder Mapping

## Overview
This project presents a stakeholder mapping analysis for the digital transformation of banking services. The objective is to identify all stakeholders involved in the implementation of digital banking solutions and understand their interests, influence, and impact on the project.

## Project
360-Degree Digitalization of Bank Services

## Primary Stakeholders
- Bank Customers
- Bank Employees
- Branch Managers
- IT Team
- Bank Management

## Secondary Stakeholders
- Reserve Bank of India (RBI)
- Government Regulators
- Payment Gateway Providers
- Cybersecurity Teams
- Software Vendors
- Cloud Service Providers

## Stakeholder Interests
- Secure Transactions
- Fast Banking Services
- Data Protection
- Regulatory Compliance
- Customer Satisfaction
- Operational Efficiency

## Benefits of Digitalization
- Improved Customer Experience
- Faster Banking Operations
- Reduced Paperwork
- Better Security
- Enhanced Accessibility
- Real-Time Banking Services
